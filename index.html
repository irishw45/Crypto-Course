Glossary

This glossary gives you quick definitions of all the key terms used throughout the course. Use it to reinforce your understanding or quickly look up concepts as you study.

  • Market: A system where assets are bought and sold.
  • Exchange: A platform that facilitates trading.
  • Volatility: The rate at which asset prices change.
  • Trader: Someone who buys and sells financial instruments.
  • Liquidity: How easily an asset can be traded without affecting its price.
  • Blockchain: A decentralized public ledger for digital transactions.
  • Token: A unit of value issued on a blockchain.
  • RSI: Relative Strength Index, a momentum indicator.
  • Stop-Loss: A price trigger to automatically close a losing trade.
  • Leverage: Using borrowed funds to increase trading exposure.
  • Cold Wallet: An offline device for storing crypto securely.
  • Phishing: A scam designed to steal private info via deception.
